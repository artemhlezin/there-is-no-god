There is no God in 2022

About this project

As a Ukrainian, I started this interactive art project after the full-scale russian invasion of Ukraine. Initially, I had considered several different concepts. Wheat sprouts growing from blood, or maybe dried blood that would have been replaced by green grass.

After the recent news from Bucha, Hostomel, and Irpin, there will be no more grass. Only blood and pain. They will never dry out, never disappear.

There are mutilated corpses of civilians on the streets and in mass graves. Thousands of Ukrainian citizens were tortured and killed by the russian army. The russians have hastily burned the bodies of raped children and women. Not sure if they were trying to cover up their war crimes, or if it was just done for "entertainment". They killed everyone, even pets, for no reason. This is not a war. This is a genocide.

And we continue to receive horrifying messages from Mariupol and other cities. The situation is even worse there.
